WHY US

At Origins Tribe, we believe that an individual's switch to a more sustainable lifestyle is precursor to achieving societal sustainablility--and the products he or she uses should reflect this. We hope that by selling a varity of products that are environmentally friendly and beautiful, we can convince people to make that switch without sacrificing what they love

OUR MISSION

Our moto is "Live Happier. Make the Planet Greener." We want to make people feel good about doing good, without any compromise on quality.

WHO ARE WE

Origin tribe is a community of people who share sustainable values. We are also aa marketplace offering 100% sustainable products for gifting, events and everyday living.
